Welcome!

I am a data scientist with a deep passion for storytelling through data. I believe that data and language are two of the most powerful tools we have to shape decisions, spark change, and improve lives.

By day, I specialize in extracting insights from complex datasets, building predictive models, and designing interactive dashboards that help organizations make smarter decisions. I’m especially drawn to end-to-end data projects—from data collection and cleaning to model building and insight delivery. One area I particularly enjoy is customer segmentation, where data meets real-world impact.

At night, I shift into creative and learning mode — reading fiction books, writing articles, experimenting with personal projects, and tackling coding challenges on platforms like LeetCode and Kaggle. I use these experiences not only to sharpen my skills but also to give back to the community through blog posts, GitHub repositories, and open-source starter kits for aspiring data scientists.

Much of my journey has been self-taught—through online courses (Coursera, edX), eBooks, and platforms like FreeCodeCamp. I’m grateful for the educators who’ve made this possible and passionate about continuing that legacy by making learning resources accessible to others.

I hold a Postgraduate Diploma in Data Science and bring hands-on experience in tools like Python, SQL, Tableau, Power BI, and machine learning frameworks. My goal is simple: to turn data into decisions and help organizations and individuals unlock their full potential through analytics.

Feel free to explore my projects and connect with me if you’d like to collaborate!
